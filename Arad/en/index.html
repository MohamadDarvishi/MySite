Arad font

Arabic(Persian) font

Download

Version 2.1.0

“Arad” is an open-source typeface designed specifically for text, featuring a geometric structure and a digital tone. Arad is designed in 10 weights and three-dots style, and according to the structure and multiple weights, it is suitable for texts and stands out in headlines. Kanit font is used for English and small changes have been made in its structure.
